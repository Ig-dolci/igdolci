About Me

I am a researcher working on computational methods for scientific computing, with a focus on numerical simulations, adjoint methods, and optimisation techniques. My work involves developing and applying differentiation algorithms for solving complex engineering problems using the open-source frameworks Pyadjoint and Firedrake.

My research focuses on computational mathematics applied to inverse problems, optimisation, and control techniques, with particular emphasis on algorithmic differentiation for adjoint-based gradient computations. I develop efficient numerical methods for wave propagation, full waveform inversion, and fluid dynamics problems, leveraging high-order finite element methods and automatic differentiation to enable gradient-based optimisation at scale.
